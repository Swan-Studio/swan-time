Swan Time

A floating menubar timer that posts to your personal Monday tracker board. Built for the Swan team — log time in five seconds without leaving what you're doing.

Download for Mac ↓ latest

  1. Open the .dmg, drag Swan Time into Applications

    First launch may show "macOS can't verify the developer." If so, right-click the app → Open → Open. You'll only need to do this once.

  2. Sign in with your Monday account

    Use the Swan email you log into Monday with. The app finds your personal "[Name] Time Tracker" board automatically — if it can't, you'll get a picker.

  3. Press ⌘ ⇧ T to summon the widget

    Type what you're working on, pick client + division + category, hit Start. Stop & log when done. The Today view shows what you've logged. Settings → enable AI for auto-categorisation and natural-language batch entry.
